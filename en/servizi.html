Services

Residenza Vignale combines the charm of a historic house with modern technology.

It is thus available for several types of events: conferences, workshops, business meetings as well as private receptions, weddings, exhibitions and photo shootings.

Facilities provided

  • Air Conditioning

  • Telephone line

  • WI FI

  • Fax and copier

  • Equipped cloakroom

Additional services

  • Florists

  • Entertainments

  • Decorations and furnishings

  • *Catering - non exclusive third-party suppliers

  • Overhead projector

  • Teleconference

  • Simultaneous translation

  • Audio Recording

  • Microphones

  • Sound system
